History of Toblerone

1867

HOW EVERYTHING
HAS STARTED

Everything begun when Jean Tobler received a settlement permit in Bern. He started working in the Kuentz pastry shop in Martkgasse in Bern, but when Kuentz fell ill, Tobler took over the business. Because the location didn't fit him, in 1868 he opens his “Confiserie Spéciale” on the first floor of a new building on Mittelstrasse in the Längassquartier. In the shop, Jean Tobler is producing chocolate products from semi-finished products that he received from other well-known manufactures.

1899

JEAN TOBLER STARTS INDUSTRIAL CHOCOLATE PRODUCTION IN BERN

Because of the huge demand for Tobler's chocolate, he decides to open his own chocolate factory. Together with his sons, he starts 'Fabrique de Chocolat Berne, Tobler & Cie'.

1908

THEODOR TOBLER AND EMIL BAUMANN INVENT THE TOBLERONE

The unique chcoloate has been created by son of Jean Tobler - Theodor Tobler, and production manager and Tobler's cousin - Emil Baumann. The 'Toblerone' stands for the name 'Tobler' and Italian word 'Torrone', which is the name for honey-almond nougat.

1920

THE BERN BEAR COMES ON THE PACK

The eagle disappeares from Tobleron packages, and the Bern's heraldic animal - the Bern bear takes his place.

1970

THE MATTERHORN APPEARS ON THE PACKAGE

The Matterhorn - the natural symbol of Switzerland, appears on the package so far only in 1970. It's not on the front yet, but only on the two sides.

1985

MOVE OF PRODUCTION TO BERN BRÜNNEN

Being a part of a major Swiss project, a powerful chocolate factory is built in the west of the city of Bern. Today, it is still the one and only Toblerone factory, where all procucts are being made and exported all over the world.

2000

THE NEW, REDESIGNED LOGO - THE BERNESE BEAR AND THE MATTERHORN

The blue and white Toblerone logo disappears. Now, the Matterhorn is in its place on the packaging. Moreover, in the shapes of the mountain, there is the Bern bear hidden.

2008

100TH BIRTHDAY OF TOBLERONE

Today, Toblerone is definitely one of the global brands. People can eat our delicious chocolate oall over the world. But wherever would they eat it, they have confidence that their chocolate was produced in the one and only Swiss factory in Bern.
